# Gabe Rides - Sponsorship Website

Hi! I’m Gabe Karekinian, a 12–13 year old rider currently on a mountain bike, planning to get my first dirt bike by April 2026/2027.  

This website showcases sponsorship opportunities for local businesses at Hartley Vale Holiday Farm. Businesses can sponsor me by providing:  

- Stickers for my jersey and bike  
- A full branded jersey  
- Full bike sponsorship  

I wear all sponsored gear during rides at the farm and share photos online.
